T01 - FIRST PROJECT

T01 IN A NUTSHELL

Process

Our group of ten came together with a shared enthusiasm for creating a parody of Formula 1. The name “Formula 10” felt like a natural choice, reflecting both the project's theme and our team size. From the start, we aimed to craft an engaging and humorous experience, with music playing a pivotal role in setting the tone. We initially stumbled upon a mariachi band cover of the official Formula 1 theme song, which felt like the perfect match for our concept.

Solution

When we realized we couldn't obtain the necessary permissions to use the mariachi band cover, we had to think on our feet. Rather than compromising the overall quality, we pivoted to finding an alternative. After exploring different options, we discovered a website offering music with a similar vibe to our original choice. The track we selected not only complemented the project but also came with the appropriate licensing, ensuring we could use it legally in our work.

Learning

This experience reinforced the importance of flexibility and problem-solving in creative projects. It highlighted the challenges of securing copyrighted materials and the value of seeking alternative solutions that align with the project's vision. We learned to navigate licensing processes, adapt to unforeseen challenges, and maintain focus on the end goal. Ultimately, the project became a testament to teamwork and resourcefulness, skills that will serve us in future endeavors.
